## 🧑‍💻 About Me

Hi, I'm **BZY**, an Algorithm Engineer at **Hangzhou Guangli Technology Co., Ltd.**, focused on the development of lightweight **Visual-Inertial Navigation Systems (VINS)**.

- 🎓 **M.Eng. in Control Science and Engineering** — *Hangzhou Dianzi University* (2024)
  - Researched **RFS-SLAM** end-to-end, from **GM-PHD-PMAU-SLAM** to **IEKF-PHD-SLAM** with multi-sensor fusion, covering both theory and engineering.
- 🎓 **B.Eng. in Mechatronic Engineering** — *Ningbo Tech University* (2021)
  - Co-founded a STEM club and competed in robotics competitions, owning the control stack: **motion modeling, cascade PID, and path planning**.

> *This is the place where I open-source stuff.*
